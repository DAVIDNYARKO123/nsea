The 2026 Research Projects

The Summer Undergraduate AI Research Institute (SAIRI) is a flagship program of the Center for Equitable AI & Machine Learning Systems (CEAMLS) at Morgan State University. Over the summer, undergraduate researchers from institutions across the country work in teams alongside faculty and graduate mentors on applied AI research. Each team presents its findings at the NSEA 2026 symposium.
