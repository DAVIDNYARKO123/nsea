About NSEA 2026

Leading the conversation on equitable artificial intelligence

The Center for Equitable AI & Machine Learning Systems (CEAMLS) at Morgan State University hosts the National Symposium on Equitable AI each year. This premier event brings together technologists, researchers, policymakers, and stakeholders for critical reflection on AI's past, present, and future.

CEAMLS serves as a crucial resource promoting AI awareness, developing K-12 AI curriculum, providing research opportunities, and fostering a diverse tech ecosystem through novel research.
