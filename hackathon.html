Important information for all participants

  • All solutions must address equitable AI challenges

  • Use of pre-built APIs and frameworks is allowed
  • Final submission deadline: June 16, 6:00 PM EDT

  • Must demonstrate clear bias mitigation or equity enhancement
  • Include data sources and methodology documentation

  • Consider ethical implications and potential harm
  • Solutions should be scalable and practical
  • Must align with NSEA 2026 equitable AI theme

üìãSubmission Format

  • 5-minute video demonstration
  • Source code repository (GitHub preferred)
  • Technical documentation (max 10 pages)
  • Live presentation to judges (10 minutes + Q&A)
  • Business/impact plan (optional but recommended)
  • All submissions must be in English

üö´Prohibited Content

  • Solutions that increase bias or discrimination
