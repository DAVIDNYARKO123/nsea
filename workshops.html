Aubrée “Brée” Nachelle Hill

Founder, Brée Nachelle Innovations
Author of Aligned, Not Automated

Workshop

Aligned, Not Automated: Designing AI Systems That Preserve Human Agency

Abstract

As AI rapidly transforms how we work, create, and make decisions, the conversation has become dominated by efficiency. But what happens when optimization comes at the expense of discernment, creativity, and human connection?

In this engaging session, founder and author Aubrée “Brée” Nachelle Hill introduces a practical framework for building AI-assisted systems that amplify human intelligence rather than replace it. Drawing from her experience in digital strategy, community building, mindfulness, and emerging technology, Brée explores how individuals and organizations can adopt AI in ways that reduce burnout, strengthen decision-making, and preserve human agency.

Attendees will leave with actionable tools for designing workflows that are not only efficient, but aligned.

About the Presenter

Brée Nachelle is what happens when a mindfulness facilitator, AI practitioner, digital strategist, and pop-culture enthusiast find a Wi-Fi signal. She is the founder of Brée Nachelle Innovations, where she helps creatives align their presence with their purpose—without burning out or blending in.

She is the author of Aligned, Not Automated, creator of the Mindful Moments newsletter, founder of Build in Beta for the AI Curious, and head instigator of the Synergy Squad, an accountability collective for soft hustlers with big visions and even bigger hearts.

Her two-decade career includes serving as a digital strategist at NASA, where she helped bring the universe to newsfeeds and timelines around the world. Today, she blends mindful practices with AI-powered strategy, teaching creators how to BREATHE through their content plan, automate their brilliance, and build sustainable momentum in both work and life.

When she's not coaching or speaking, you can usually find Brée sipping peppermint tea, crafting lead magnets in Notion, or plotting the next creative revolution—likely accompanied by a meme that's equal parts funny and healing.

Adalbert “Addy” De La Cruz

Founder, RapidLabz
Creator of Vibe Connect

Workshop

Building Sustainable Software with a Product Lens

A Hands-On Lovable Workshop

Abstract

In a world where software can be spun up in minutes, the discipline of building sustainable products is too often lost to trend-driven “vibe coding.” This hands-on, 2-hour workshop reframes AI-powered building through a product strategy lens—teaching participants not just how to prompt their way to an app, but how to build software people actually want to use.

Using Lovable, participants will work through the fundamentals of product thinking—user stories, user scenarios, and designing for real user needs—while building a functional application live. The session emphasizes the human-AI collaboration at the heart of modern product development, directly aligning with this year's symposium theme.

Whether building consumer-led or enterprise software, attendees will leave with a repeatable, programmatic framework for turning AI tools into sustainable products rather than one-off demos.

What participants get

  • A hands-on, build-along session using Lovable
  • 100 free Lovable credits (~$20 value) per participant
  • A practical product framework they can apply immediately

Requirements: Participants should bring their own laptops. Wi-Fi will be provided on-site.

About the Presenter

Adalbert “Addy” De La Cruz is an AI educator, product strategist, and community builder helping people turn ideas into real products through AI. Over the past several years, he has worked across product, operations, marketing, and emerging technology roles at organizations including Google, DraftKings, Deloitte, Morgan Stanley, and leading Web3 startups, building products, partnerships, and programs at the intersection of technology and innovation.

As the Founder of RapidLabz and creator of Vibe Connect, Addy has taught more than 1,500 aspiring builders, entrepreneurs, and professionals how to leverage AI, product thinking, and vibe coding to launch businesses, automate workflows, and bring ideas to life. His workshops, accelerators, and community programs have partnered with organizations such as Coinbase, Base, Lyft, Lovable, and universities across the country.

Today, Addy focuses on the future of AI-powered product development, agentic systems, and digital entrepreneurship. Through speaking engagements, educational programs, and community-led events, he helps founders, creators, and organizations navigate the rapidly evolving AI landscape while building products that create meaningful impact.
