Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the conference? ▼

Registration is available on our main website. Choose your registration category (Student, Academic, Industry, or Virtual) and complete the online form. Early bird rates are available until April 30, 2026.

What is the deadline for paper submissions? ▼

Abstract submissions are due February 15, 2026, and full papers are due March 1, 2026. All submissions must be made through our EasyChair portal following the ACM conference format.

Is financial assistance available for students? ▼

Yes! We offer travel grants and reduced registration fees for students. HBCU students and underrepresented minorities are especially encouraged to apply. Contact us at ceamls@morgan.edu for more information.

Can I attend virtually? ▼

Absolutely! We offer virtual attendance with live streaming of keynotes, interactive breakout sessions, digital networking, and access to all recorded content. Virtual registration is $199.

What COVID-19 safety measures will be in place? ▼

We follow all Morgan State University and local health guidelines. Current information about safety protocols will be communicated to registered attendees as the conference approaches.
