NSEA 2025

AI in Practice: Impacts, Risks, and Opportunities

April 5-6, 2025 | Earl G. Graves School of Business, Morgan State University

Keynote Speakers

  • Arjune Sen - Oxford University
  • Fay Cobb Payton - Rutgers U. / NC State U.
  • Sina Fazelpour - Northeastern University
  • Agus Sudjianto - Wells Fargo / UNC Charlotte

Topics Covered

  • • AI & Society, Medicine, Ethics
  • • AI & Education, Environment
  • • Bias Mitigation & Fairness
  • • Explainability, Safety & Reliability
  • • AI & Decision Sciences, Philosophy

About: The 2025 symposium examined practical AI applications across healthcare, education, and social systems. Featured 60+ presentations, poster sessions, and panel discussions from researchers at Howard University, Johns Hopkins, Princeton, Rutgers, and other leading institutions.

NSEA 2024

Elevate Equity: Reimagining AI's Potential

2024 | Morgan State University, Baltimore, MD

Keynote Speakers

  • Rachele Hendricks-Sturrup - Duke University
  • Michael Running Wolf - First Languages AI Reality
  • John Pasmore & Chuck Faush - Latimer / Miles College
  • Hal Daumé III - University of Maryland
  • Solon Barocas - Microsoft / Cornell Univ.

Topics Covered

  • • Equitable AI in Law and Policy
  • • Equitable AI in Research
  • • Algorithmic Fairness
  • • AI in Engineering, Industry, Government

About: The inaugural symposium established foundations for dialogue on fairness and ethics in AI. Featured special guests from U.S. Department of Homeland Security: Fayrouz Saad (Assistant Secretary, OPE) and Shoba Sivaprasad Wadhia (Officer, CLCR) discussing national security, equity, and AI.
